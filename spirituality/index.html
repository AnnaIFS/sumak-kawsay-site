Sumak Kawsay

Spirituality

Andean spirituality as daily practice. Walking with the Great Spirit, Pachamama, and the ancestors.

Daily practice

Think well, feel well, act well.

The spirituality of Sumak Kawsay is not a belief separated from daily life. It is life itself, attended to with reverence.

Each day we recognize and share spiritual experiences that shape our habits. Our path cultivates spirituality as continuous action rather than belief alone. This is a path that requires conscious daily practice.
